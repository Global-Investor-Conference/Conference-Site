About Us

The Global Investor Conference is a complimentary event designed to bring learning and networking opportunities to the Linqto Community of Investors. Speakers are not compensated nor do companies pay Linqto to participate in the program. Linqto is dedicated to making sure that these programs are educational and informative in nature and often reveal opportunities in the market for potential investors to explore more on their own.

Information

Linqto's live and virtual quarterly Global Investor Conference (GIC) provides our clients and guests with a unique opportunity to learn about the latest trends in private equity and venture investments. The GIC is truly global, typically featuring diverse viewpoints from Europe, the Americas, and APAC throughout the course of the event.

Welcome to the Biggest
Private Investing Conference

Linqto's live and virtual quarterly Global Investor Conference (GIC) provides our clients and guests with a unique opportunity to learn about the latest trends in private equity and venture investments. The GIC is truly global, typically featuring diverse viewpoints from Europe, the Americas, and APAC throughout the course of the event.

Why you should Join The Global Investor Conference

World Class Speakers

The Global Investor Conference brings together some of the brightest minds and most influential professionals in digital assets.

Best Experience

2022’s GIC will see us finally all meet in person, in captivating Lisbon! We will have discussions, Q&As, roundtables, and masterclasses about the world of investing and technology.

Networking

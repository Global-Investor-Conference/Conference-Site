welcome

Welcome to a Unique
Private Investing Conference

Engage, Educate, Empower - that’s what GIC is here for. There’s a surplus of knowledge, expertise, conflicting opinions, which make it hard to know where to start, let alone navigate the storm that is today and the promised land that is tomorrow.

On The 6th, 7th and 8th of December we’re taking to our virtual Global Investor Conference (GIC) stage and bringing together the best and the brightest to sift through this noise. From tokenization, to post-platform economies, from self-sovereign identities to DAOs and yes, crypto regulation. We’ve got it here for YOUR Linqto eyes. We’ll go further and discern the very infrastructure of the promised Web3 and its foundation, decentralisation.

We’ll scan new technologies and de-evangelize them, meaning we’ll take narratives and ideas of the future, be it Web3, or AI, and see if they hold up to scrutiny. We’ll contextualise technical knowledge to make it accessible to ALL. We’ll open up the floor to all opinions. And we want yours.

We’re here to help avoid those that slap ‘open code’ and ‘decentralization’ onto something. That’s tech solutions and that’s an easy to fall into trap. We’re not falling down it. Who is this for? This is for investors who want to make informed investment decisions. This is for those who want to gather a 360-degree view. We’ve identified the brilliant minds, leading thinkers and investors to have in-depth, incredibly important discussions, you just need to attend and prepare to learn.
